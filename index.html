Take Notes Faster

Write, edit and save your ideas to Markdown files without ever leaving your keyboard. QuickDown is built from the ground up to feel and work like a pen-and-paper notepad.

Capture a new idea with ONE GLOBAL HOTKEY that works anywhere and anytime. Your cursor is immediately focused to go from idea to typing your first letter in 0.42ms or less.

Save To Markdown

Your notes are saved locally as plain text Markdown files. As such, they're fully backup-able and future-proof. You can store them in any folder you want including iCloud, Google drive, DropBox and OneDrive.

Save time with custom markdown templates for new notes. Pre-fill your note with live-variables like today's date, time, an auto-incrementing ID. And control exactly where you want your cursor to be placed when you start writing.

Keep Your Focus

No need to switch to a behemoth Markdown editor just to take a quick note. To keep you focused and avoid distraction, QuickDown is intentionally bare-bones and only appears when you really need it.

It is a lightweight companion to your preferred knowledge base such as Obsidian, LogSeq, Microsoft OneNote, VS Code, TiddlyWiki, Zettlr, Vim, Emacs, iA Writer, MacDown and any other plain text editor, really.
