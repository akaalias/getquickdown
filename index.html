QuickDown is a blazing-fast, tiny and highly customizable notepad that sits on top of local plain text Markdown files.

It is an ideal complement for those who want to quickly capture ideas in the moment and later flesh them out with Obsidian, LogSeq, VS Code, iA Writer, Zettlr, TiddlyWiki, Org-Mode and other knowledge bases

Get QuickDown v2.2 for macOS

"Keyboard-First"

Just like a real notepad, QuickDown is built from the ground up to feel like pen-and-paper. To capture a new idea, you have one global hotkey that works anywhere and anytime: Option-Command-N

Your cursor is immediately focused to go from idea to typing your first letter in 0.42ms or less. Write, edit and save your ideas to Markdown files without ever leaving your keyboard.

"Local Markdown"

Upon saving, your notes are stored locally as plain text Markdown files in any folder you choose.

As plain text files they can be directly saved to your iCloud, Google drive, DropBox and OneDrive.

To save you time, new notes can be fully customized with templates. Include your preferred structure with YAML frontmatter, headers, lists, quotes, placeholders, tags or anything else you know you need.

"Note-Taking"

QuickDown is intentionally tiny and so simple that it will not add to your cognitive load. No need to launch or switch to a behemoth markdown editor just to take a quick note!

It is a perfect complement to your existing knowledge base or writing workflow. From here you can proceed to weave it into your knowledge base such as Obsidian, LogSeq, VS Code, iA Writer, Zettlr, TiddlyWiki, Org-Mode.
