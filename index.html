Take Notes Faster

Write, edit and save your ideas without ever leaving your keyboard. QuickDown is built from the ground up to be keyboard-first to feel like a pen-and-paper notepad.

Capture a new idea with ONE GLOBAL HOTKEY that works anywhere and anytime. Your cursor is immediately focused to go from idea to typing your first letter in 0.42ms or less.

Save To Markdown

Your notes are saved locally as plain text Markdown files. As such, they're fully backup-able and future-proof. You can store them in any folder you want, like iCloud, Google Drive and OneDrive.

Save time with custom markdown templates for new notes. Control your cursor-placement and automatically pre-fill them with live-variables such as today's date, time, and an auto-incremented ID.

Keep Your Focus

No need to switch to a behemoth Markdown editor just to take a quick note. QuickDown's "Less, but better" approach helps you take notes, stay focused and avoid distractions.

It's an ideal companion to your preferred knowledge base such as Obsidian, LogSeq, Microsoft OneNote, VS Code, TiddlyWiki, Zettlr, Vim, Emacs, iA Writer, MacDown and any other plain text editor, really.
