QuickDown is a blazing-fast, no-frills, tiny and customizable notepad that sits on top of local plain text Markdown files

It is ideal for people who want to quickly capture ideas NOW and then flesh them out LATER with their personal writing-process and text-based knowledge bases like Obsidian, LogSeq, VS Code, iA Writer, Zettlr, TiddlyWiki, Org-Mode

Get QuickDown for macOS (.dmg)

"Keyboard-First"

Just like a real notepad, QuickDown is built from the ground up to feel like pen-and-paper. To capture a new idea, you have one global hotkey that works anywhere and anytime.

Your cursor is immediately focused to go from idea to typing your first letter in 0.42ms or less. Write, edit and save your ideas to Markdown files without ever leaving your keyboard.

"Local Markdown"

Upon saving, your notes are stored locally as plain text Markdown files in any folder you want. As plain text files they can be directly synced to your iCloud, Google drive, DropBox and OneDrive.

Save time with custom templates for new notes. Start with your preferred document structure. Pre-fill your template with today's date, time and clipboard-content. Control exactly where you want your cursor to be placed when you start writing.

"Note-Taking"

QuickDown is intentionally bare-bones and tiny to keep you focused and avoid distraction. No need to launch or switch to a behemoth markdown editor just to take a quick note!

It is a lightweight companion to your existing writing-process and text-based knowledge base like Obsidian, LogSeq, VS Code, iA Writer, Zettlr, TiddlyWiki, and Org-Mode.
